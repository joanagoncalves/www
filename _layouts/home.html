Intro

I am an ERCIM / Marie Curie postdoctoral fellow at the Centre for Computer Science and Mathematics in the Netherlands (CWI). My background is in computer science and I do research in applied machine learning and data mining to tackle interesting problems in diverse application domains, with an emphasis on molecular biology and functional genomics. I specialise in combinatorial pattern mining for biclustering and triclustering of time series data.
