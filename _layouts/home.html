Short biography

I do research in algorithms, applied machine learning and data mining to answer relevant questions in diverse domains, with an emphasis on molecular biology and functional genomics for cancer research. I specialise in combinatorial pattern mining for biclustering and triclustering of time series data.

Position

I am a postdoc with Lodewyk Wessels at the Delft University of Technology (PRB / TUDelft) in Delft, and The Netherlands Cancer Institute (CCB / NKI) in Amsterdam. In this position, I also seek to create synergies between the two groups through the initiation of collaborative projects.
